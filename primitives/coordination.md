# Primitive — Coordination

## Definition

Coordination is the recurring bottleneck that emerges whenever Builders successfully scale a system.

It is not a one-time problem — it reappears at every new level of scale.

---

## Why Coordination Keeps Recurring

Every time Builders solve a scarcity:
- Systems become more powerful
- More people and components are involved
- Complexity increases faster than alignment

This creates a coordination gap that People Movers must close.

---

## Coordination Through the Ages

| After This Build | Coordination Problem That Emerged |
|---|---|
| Farming | How to govern land and surplus |
| Cities | How to rule large populations |
| Factories | How to manage labor at scale |
| Global infrastructure | How to coordinate nations and trade |
| The Internet | How to direct attention and behavior |
| AI systems | How to align human + machine action |

---

## The Current Coordination Crisis

Today's coordination failure has three layers:

**1. Clarity — What matters?**
- We don't lack information
- We lack: prioritization, meaning, clear goals
- Too many options → paralysis

**2. Coordination — Who does what?**
- Modern systems are massively interconnected
- But alignment is fragile, incentives are mismatched
- Small coordination failures → huge inefficiency

**3. Execution Alignment — Actually doing it**
- Ideas: available
- Tools: available
- Resources: available
- Sustained, aligned execution: scarce

---

## Why Attention and Intelligence Are Not the Root Constraint

These are symptoms, not the bottleneck:

| Candidate | Why It Doesn't Qualify |
|---|---|
| Attention | Personal optimization problem, not universal system bottleneck |
| Intelligence | Increasingly abundant via AI, not strictly scarce |

The real scarcity is **coherent direction and coordination of action**.

---

## Key Insight

> We no longer struggle to produce.
> We struggle to agree, prioritize, and act coherently.

---

## Relationship to Other Primitives

- Coordination is what breaks after Builders succeed
- People Movers exist specifically to solve coordination
- Scarcity of coordination defines the current age
