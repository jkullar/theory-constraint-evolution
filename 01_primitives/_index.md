# Primitives — Reading Order

A **primitive** is the smallest indivisible unit of the theory — a force or role that cannot be broken down further without losing its meaning. Primitives are not historical events or specific inventions; they are recurring patterns that appear in every age. Together they form the engine of the constraint loop.

The primitives are the core moving parts of the constraint loop. Read them in this order to follow the logic:

```
[ SCARCITY ] → [ BUILDERS ] → [ COORDINATION ] → [ COORDINATOR ] → (loop)
```
