# Model — The Core Constraint Loop

## The Loop

```
Scarcity
  → Builders solve it
    → Scale increases
      → Coordination breaks
        → People Movers organize
          → New Scarcity emerges
            → (repeat)
```

---

## Core Claim

Human progress is driven by solving the dominant scarcity, which creates new systems, which then require coordination, which then exposes the next bottleneck.

---

## Two Permanent Forces

| Force | Role | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Builder | Creates systems | Capability |
| People Mover | Aligns humans | Coherence |

These never disappear — they evolve with each age.

---

## The Loop in Detail

**Step 1: Builders win**
- Railroads built
- Internet created
- AI developed
- → Systems become more powerful

**Step 2: Coordination breaks**
- Too many options
- Too many users
- Too much complexity
- → Need better People Movers

**Step 3: People Movers win**
- Governments, corporations, platforms
- → They organize usage

**Step 4: New bottleneck appears**
- Systems become inefficient again
- → Builders step in again

---

## The Scarcity Shift Pattern

Scarcities become progressively less physical and more abstract:

```
Physical (food, land, energy)
  → Mechanical (labor, production)
    → Digital (information)
      → Systemic (alignment, coordination)
        → Abstract (meaning, direction)
```

---

## Meta-Model (5 Laws)

1. Scarcity defines the age
2. Builders remove the constraint
3. Scale creates chaos
4. People Movers restore order
5. New scarcity emerges (less physical, more abstract)

---

## Final Insight

> We are transitioning from:
> World of scarcity of resources
>
> to:
> World of scarcity of coherence
>
> And in the future:
> Scarcity of meaning and direction
