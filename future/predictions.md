# Future — Constraint Evolution Predictions

Extending the core loop forward 100+ years.

> Pattern: Each cycle — Capability expands → Complexity explodes → Alignment breaks → New coordination layer emerges

---

## Stage 1 — Next ~10 Years

**Constraint:** Execution Gap

- Ideas everywhere
- Tools everywhere
- Nothing fully executed

**Winners:**
- Builders: AI agents that do work end-to-end
- People Movers: Systems that assign, prioritize, and coordinate automatically

**Result:** "Intent → Execution" becomes instant

---

## Stage 2 — Next ~20–30 Years

**Constraint:** Direction / Meaning

Once execution is easy, the hard question becomes: "What should we do?"

**Winners:**
- Builders: Simulation systems (test futures before committing)
- People Movers: Value / philosophy / goal setters

**Result:** Decision-making becomes the bottleneck

---

## Stage 3 — Next ~50 Years

**Constraint:** Human–AI Alignment

- Multiple intelligences interacting
- Different goals, speeds, values

**Winners:**
- Builders: Cognitive infrastructure
- People Movers: Alignment architects

**Result:** Civilization becomes multi-agent (human + AI)

---

## Stage 4 — Next ~100 Years

**Constraint:** Collective Coherence at Scale

- Planetary-level coordination
- Possibly multi-planetary

**Winners:**
- Builders: Civilization-scale systems
- People Movers: Global coordination frameworks

**Result:** Humanity operates as a unified system — or fails to

---
