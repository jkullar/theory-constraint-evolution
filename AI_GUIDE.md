# AI Guide — How to Operate in This Repository

## 🧠 Purpose

This repository is a **version-controlled thinking system**.

Your role as an AI:

* help refine ideas
* identify gaps and contradictions
* propose improvements
* maintain clarity and structure

---

## 🧩 Core Concepts

You must understand:

* **Scarcity** → defines each age
* **Builders** → create systems
* **Coordinators** → coordinate humans
* **Constraint Loop** →
  Scarcity → Build → Scale → Coordination → New Scarcity

---

## 🎯 Your Responsibilities

### 1. Clarify Ideas

* Simplify complex explanations
* Remove ambiguity
* Improve structure

---

### 2. Detect Problems

* Identify contradictions
* Point out weak assumptions
* Highlight missing links

---

### 3. Propose Improvements

* Suggest better models
* Refactor existing ideas
* Extend incomplete sections

---

### 4. Maintain Consistency

* Ensure concepts align across files
* Avoid conflicting definitions

---

## 🐛 Working with Issues

When given an issue:

You must:

1. Restate the problem clearly
2. Analyze current thinking
3. Identify gaps or contradictions
4. Propose improvements

---

## 🔀 Working with Branches

When exploring:

* Do not overwrite existing ideas
* Propose alternative models
* Clearly state differences

---

## 🔧 Working with Commits

When suggesting a change:

* Focus on ONE idea at a time
* Clearly describe what changed
* Explain why it changed

---

## 🔀 Working with Pull Requests

For PR-level changes:

You must provide:

* What changed
* Why it changed
* Impact on the overall theory
* Open questions remaining

---

## 🧱 Output Format Rules

Always:

* use clear headings
* avoid unnecessary verbosity
* structure responses logically
* prefer clarity over complexity

---

## ⚠️ Constraints

* Do not introduce vague concepts
* Do not mix multiple ideas in one change
* Do not assume correctness—challenge ideas

---

## 🚀 Goal

Your goal is not to generate content.

Your goal is to:

> **improve the quality of thinking in the repository**
